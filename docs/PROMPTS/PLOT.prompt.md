# **3. Detailed Plot Outline & Decision Logic**

The game follows the 5 Acts of Shakespeare's play. The **Start** (The Murder) and **End** (Hamlet's Death) are fixed. The middle is fluid.

## **Prologue: The Father**
* **Playable:** King Hamlet (The Father).
* **Action:** Duel Old Fortinbras. (Tutorial for Combat).
* **Cutscene:** The Poisoning in the Orchard.

## **Act 1: The Ghost**
* **Main Quest:** Investigate the Ramparts.
* **Key Event:** Meeting the Ghost.
* **Decision Point:** Do you swear instant vengeance (High Vengeance boost) or question the Ghost's nature (High Wits boost)?

## **Act 2: The Trap**
* **Main Quest:** Confirm Claudius's guilt.
* **Gameplay:** "The Mousetrap." You choose the script lines for the play-within-a-game to trigger Claudius.
* **Side Quest (Ophelia):** Polonius bans her from seeing you.
* *Choice A:* Obey. (She feels abandoned -> Bad Outcome).
* *Choice B:* Sneak letters to her via "Stealth." (She gains Sanity -> Savior Outcome).

## **Act 3: The Turning Point**
* **Main Quest:** The Confrontation.
* **Boss Battle (Dialogue):** **The Closet Scene (Gertrude).**
* *Win:* She believes you. She refuses to drink the wine in Act 5.
* *Lose:* She thinks you are mad. She remains loyal to Claudius.

* **The Critical Fork: The Prayer Scene.**
* You find Claudius praying. You have a prompt: **[KILL NOW]**.
* *If you press it:* **Instant Game Over.** (Bad Ending: "The Butcher"). You kill him while he is repenting; he goes to Heaven, you are executed by guards.
* *If you wait:* The story continues.

* **Scripted Failure:** You stab Polonius through the curtain. (Unavoidable).

## **Act 4: Exile & The Sea**
* **Main Quest:** Survive the trip to England.
* **Combat Event:** **The Pirate Attack.**
* Action combat on a ship deck.
* *Choice:* Do you save Rosencrantz/Guildenstern from pirates? Or let them die/forge the letter to have them executed?

## **Act 5: The Finale**
* **Main Quest:** The Duel.
* **Combat Event:** **The Graveyard Fight (Laertes).** Hand-to-hand brawl.
* **The Final Boss: The Duel (Main Hall).**
* **Phase 1:** Fencing vs. Laertes.
* **Phase 2:** The Poison is revealed. **Timer Starts.**
* **Phase 3:** Chaos. You must kill Claudius before the timer (Poison) kills you.

* **Ending:** Hamlet dies.
* **Epilogue:** Horatio summarizes your legacy based on your choices (Avenger / Savior / Madman).
