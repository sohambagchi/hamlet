# **5. Equipment & Inventory**

Inventory is divided into **Physical Gear** (Combat) and **Plot Items** (Investigation).
## **A. Physical Gear (Combat Stats)**
### **Right Hand (Weapon):**
* *Steel Foil:* Starting weapon.
* *Rusty Blade:* Found in Shipwreck (Act 4). Tetanus effect (Poison).
* *Laertes’ Foil (Unbated):* Stolen in Act 5. High Crit.
### **Left Hand (Trinket):**
* *Yorick’s Skull:* (Act 5). Allows "Instant Soliloquy" (Heal Resolve in battle).
* *Ophelia’s Rue:* Boosts Max Resolve.
* *Father’s Signet:* Boosts Vengeance.
## **B. The "Rumor" System (Plot Inventory)**

Information is gathered as tangible items.
* **"The Ghost's Secret":** Obtained Act 1.
* **"The Murder Play Script":** Crafted Act 2.
* **"R&G's Death Warrant":** Forged Act 4.
* **"The Poisoned Pearl":** Evidence found in Act 5.
* *Usage:* These are "Keys" to unlock dialogue branches or "Ammo" for critical hits in arguments.
