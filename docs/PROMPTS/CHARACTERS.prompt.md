# **2. Characters**

## **Playable Character: Prince Hamlet**
* **Role:** The Solo Protagonist.
* **Appearance:** Black attire. Evolving sprite (starts clean, becomes disheveled/bloody).
* **Attributes (Stats):**
* **Vengeance (STR):** Aggression, Fencing Dmg, Intimidation.
* **Wits (INT):** Logic, Speed, Critical Hit Chance, Dialogue Puzzles.
* **Resolve (HP/SANITY):** Mental health. Drained by taking damage or using "Glitch Mode."
## **Significant NPCs (The "Bosses" of Dialogue)**
* **Claudius (The Antagonist):** High Composure, High Deception. Hard to read.
* **Gertrude (The Mother):** High Bond, Low Resolve. Can be swayed to your side.
* **Polonius (The Gatekeeper):** High Wits, Low Patience. Easily confused by "Madness."
* **Ophelia (The Victim):** Fragile. Her fate depends on your dialogue choices.
* **Laertes (The Rival):** Physical threat. The final combat boss.
* **Horatio (The Anchor):** The "Save Point." He heals your Resolve and records the "True History."
* **The Ghost (The Guide/Haunt):** Unpredictable. Appears to buff or debuff you.

### **Insignificant NPCs (The "Crowd")**
* **Courtiers/Guards:** They form the "Audience." They react to your behavior (cheering or fleeing), affecting the **Suspicion Meter**.
* **Rosencrantz & Guildenstern:** Temporary annoyances. They try to "party" with you, stealing your XP/Items until you exile them.
