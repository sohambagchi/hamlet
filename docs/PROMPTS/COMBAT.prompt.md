# **6. Combat System: "Lethal Action"**

> **Style:** Real-time, top-down, high-stakes. No turn-based menus.

## **Controls:**
* **Stick:** Move.
* **A Button:** Thrust / Attack.
* **B Button:** Sidestep / Dodge.
* **L-Trigger (Hold):** **Glitch Mode** (Bullet Time).
* Slows enemies by 50%.
* Player moves at normal speed.
* Drains Resolve rapidly.

## **Mechanics:**
* **Posture System:** Enemies block attacks. You must **Parry** (timed block) or **Lunge** (charged attack) to break Posture.
* **Lethality:** Sword hits are deadly. You take 2-3 hits max before dying.
* **The "Poison" Timer:** In the final boss fight, your Max HP slowly decreases every second (representing the poison in your veins), forcing an aggressive playstyle.

## **Enemies:**
* **Guards:** Standard spear/sword users.
* **Pirates:** Heavy axe users (Require dodging).
* **Laertes:** Mirror match. Fast, uses the same moveset as the player.
